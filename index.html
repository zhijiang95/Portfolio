Just the fact

7: The number of Academy Award winners in the past twenty years who thanked God in their acceptance speeches. 30: Who thanked Harvey Weinstein.

Harvey Weinstein was accused of sexual harassment, thus I chose the black to colour the Oscar gold award for the part of western. In the centre, there is a halo that represents the holy and pure. Which are surrounded by the black Oscar status to show there are two different power is challenging each other In the terms of font, Didot , it looks like elegant and beautiful, It looks like decorated letter, but it looks much more readable . And it still looks elegant.
Theme: My idea comes from the Oscar invite letter. The background colour want to simulate is the quality of velvet, this because the curtain of Oscar used this kind of colour and material. It will recall people’s memory.


1.3:A design defect of the Hubble Space Telescope in millimetres, costing its designers $1.5 billion to fix.

The original estimated cost for Hubble telescope is 400 million. From my perspective this is the very interesting fact that I should point out in this sheet. Because only a 1.3 millimetre defect on the mirror costs NASA 2.1 billion Australian dollar to fix . The fabrication error causes the starlight to blur because the telescope could not bring all the light to a single focal point. These two comparison would remind the public and the government to make decide wisely. The real cost of a project could me multiple times of its estimated cost. The 4 dashed line means the original cost of telescope. This fact sheet chose bold Helvetica to match the shape of the telescope, because it’s a fairly “fat” telescope. In the term of colour I chose turquoise and grey.


13/25: The portion of revenue China's cheese market generates (US $1,056 million) vs Australia's cheese market (US $2,046 million).

For the better understanding, I have rewritten the facts to 3 sentences as follows”the promotion of cheese consumption” ,“the promotion of cheese revenue” and “china vs Australia”. This because I want to compare the revenue and the consumption. Even the cheese revenue Australia generated is two times more than china, the consumption is 4times more than china((24.6 million * 11.4kg)/(1.38billion * 0.05) = 4.08 more than china. I also use 13 to represent the promotion of china cheese consumption, which ,make audience easier to notice that the per kilo cheese revenue of Australia is less than china, although the overall revenue is more than china.I use the Analogous scheme for the colour, all of these colour are used to represent the cheese theme. Exclude the background colour. I use this background colour to make the data stand out.About the china and Australia font , I design a cheese font for these two country , and also use different colour to represent different country.

2024 olympic


I choose the red blue and white ,which is the national flag colour of France. For the tower, I choose the red. Also gestalt law was used here. Tower consist of 3 main part , which can reduce audience cognitive load. Also i used red and blue white line to simulate the track. And there are athletics running and riding on that to imply there are varying competitions.
Issues: the Paris tower is too powerful and popular. I am so hard to find other element can represent Paris. Because is so classical, the tower is the only thing which is modern. I can connect it with modern olympic. It would be awkward to connect athletics with Paris theatre or arch.
